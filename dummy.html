Skill Development- An Employment Ready workforce.
Orchid Learning Centers ( OHA) skills development curriculum is beyond the conventional education which aims at creating professional skills in the students, enabling them to be ‘Achievers’ . Presently, most of the companies are experiencing a essential skills void in the job applicants which are necessary for them to hire, train and retain. Our practical and industry integrated training focuses on developing behavior and skills, which will impact all areas of students’ life, enhancing their current or future job performances. The Training programmes aims at developing students’ self confidence by enhancing their competence, improve their productivity. Depending on the career sector and profession you choose to work in , there would be specific skills, abilities and knowledge needed to perform in the job effectively. These are complemented with the essential skills for successful performance in the business environment. As a matter of fact, the employers are facing difficulty in finding workers who are trained for the jobs with requisite competencies. Hence, work skills training helps develop the job related competencies to enable the candidates excel in their career.
